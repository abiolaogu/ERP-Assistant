## 1. GTM Philosophy

Our go-to-market is built on **viral adoption within the enterprise**. When one department starts using Sovereign Assistant, neighboring departments see the productivity gains and request access. Our 30-day free trial removes procurement friction for initial adoption, and our per-user pricing makes it trivial to expand. The result: 148% NRR driven primarily by organic user expansion.

## 5. Marketing Strategy

### 5.1 Positioning

**For** enterprise operations leaders **who** are frustrated that employees waste hours navigating complex ERP systems, **Sovereign Assistant** is the AI copilot **that** lets every employee query, generate documents, and automate tasks across all business modules using natural language, **unlike** Microsoft Copilot and Glean which only work with documents and search, not enterprise operations data.

### 5.2 Category: "Enterprise Operations Copilot"

We are creating the "Enterprise Operations Copilot" category -- distinct from general AI assistants (Copilot), enterprise search (Glean), and IT automation (Moveworks). Our category is defined by three criteria:
1. Native access to operational data (ERP, not documents)
2. Action capability (execute workflows, not just search)
3. Domain intelligence (understands business processes, not just language)

For a 2,000-employee enterprise at $20/user/month ($480K/year):

- Document automation: 500 docs/month x $15 savings = **$90K/year**
- Agent automation: 200 tasks/month x $25 savings = **$60K/year**
- **Total annual savings: $6.3M**
- **ROI: 13.1x** ($6.3M savings / $480K cost)
