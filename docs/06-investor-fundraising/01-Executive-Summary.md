## The Opportunity

Enterprise employees spend 28% of their workweek searching for information across disconnected systems. In organizations running 24+ ERP modules, answering a simple cross-departmental question -- "Which departments are over budget on headcount?" -- requires logging into two systems, running separate queries, and manually correlating results. This takes 47 minutes on average. Sovereign Assistant answers it in 8 seconds using natural language.

Sovereign Assistant is an AI copilot for enterprise operations. It provides natural language access to all 24 ERP modules, generates documents from templates populated with live data, summarizes meetings, automates repetitive tasks via AI agents, and maintains a RAG-powered knowledge base that captures institutional knowledge. Every employee, from the CEO to a warehouse worker, interacts with their business systems through conversation.

## Market

The enterprise AI assistant market is valued at **$12 billion** (2025) and growing at **32% CAGR**, reaching $48 billion by 2030. Three forces drive this growth:

1. **Generative AI adoption:** 78% of enterprises plan to deploy AI assistants by 2027 (Gartner)
2. **Productivity mandate:** Post-pandemic efficiency pressure drives automation investment
3. **ERP complexity:** Average enterprise uses 24+ business applications; natural language is the universal interface

## Differentiation

Sovereign Assistant is the **only AI copilot natively integrated with a complete ERP ecosystem**. While Microsoft Copilot operates on Office documents, Google Duet on Workspace, and Glean on search, none of them can execute workflows across finance, HR, inventory, manufacturing, and procurement in a single conversation.

**Four structural advantages:**
1. **24-module native access** via shared Redpanda event bus and GraphQL federation
2. **Action, not just answers** -- AI agents execute multi-step workflows (create PO, submit expense, onboard employee)
3. **Enterprise-grade grounding** -- every response cites its source module, entity, and timestamp
4. **Compliance-aware** -- RBAC-enforced responses ensure users only see data they are authorized to access
