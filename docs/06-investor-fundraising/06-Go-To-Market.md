## 1. GTM Strategy: Privacy-Led Enterprise Sales

Unlike consumer AI assistants that compete on features alone, Sovereign Assistant leads with **privacy as a feature**. Our GTM targets the 71% of CIOs who cite data privacy as their #1 blocker for AI adoption.

**Primary message:** "Enterprise AI that never sees your data."

## 5. Competitive Positioning

### Against Microsoft Copilot ($30/user/month + M365 license required)
**Message:** "Same AI capability. Your data stays in your hands."
- Copilot requires M365 E3/E5 ($36/user/month base) PLUS $30/user = $66/user minimum
- Sovereign Assistant: $15-45/user, no platform prerequisite
- Copilot sends data to Microsoft; we never see your data

### Against Glean ($4.6B valuation)
**Message:** "Glean searches. We search, answer, and act."
- Glean is search only; we add NLQ, workflows, meetings, agents
- Glean is cloud-only; we offer on-premises
- Glean costs $15-25/user for search alone; we include search in our base tier
