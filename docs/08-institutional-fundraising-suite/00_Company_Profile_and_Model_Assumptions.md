# ERP-Assistant Company Profile and Model Assumptions

## Company Details

- Company Description: Cross-system enterprise assistant for guided execution, search, and action completion
- Target Market: Knowledge workers and operations teams across multi-business-unit enterprises
- Business Model: Seat-based subscription + workflow action volume + enterprise add-ons
- Funding Ask: $20M
- Geography: US, UK

## Category-King Positioning Statement

ERP-Assistant is positioned to become the category-defining operating platform in its domain by combining product depth, governance-grade controls, and financially disciplined scale.

## Formula Logic

- Revenue = Subscription + Usage + Services
- Gross Profit = Revenue x Gross Margin
- EBITDA = Gross Profit - Opex
- Free Cash Flow = EBITDA - Capex - Working Capital Delta
- LTV = (ACV x Gross Margin) / Logo Churn
- CAC Payback (months) = CAC / ((ACV x Gross Margin) / 12)
- MOIC = Investor Proceeds / Invested Capital
- IRR = (MOIC^(1/5)) - 1
